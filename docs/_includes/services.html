About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I appreciate you making it this far into learning about me, my skills, and what makes me unique. Since I was a child, it has been my dream to learn how to code. However, I struggled to take to the online math lessons that were a part of my homeschool curriculum, so I gave up my dreams of programming and pursued art. Once I began college, I learned that I actually am quite good at math and programming, and that I just needed a teacher to foster that within me. Now my dream is to combine my art and design skills with my programming skills to create visually appealing and sleek designs.

Of course, I enjoy more than just UI/UX design, one of my favorite courses I have taken involved creating a pseudo x86 assembler and writing assembly code for it to run. I greatly enjoy both the visual design aspect of programming, and the puzzle-solving aspect of data manipulation and other backend logic. These skills combined with my theoretical knowledge of programming I have accumulated over the course of my education have made me a well-rounded developer capable of taking on a wide breadth of challenges.

My aim with this portfolio is to give you an overview of the sorts of projects I have worked on and the skills I have acquired from working on those projects. On this page you will find descriptions of the work I have done, along with my resume, some personal hobbies of mine, and my contact info.
