Interests

Here are some of the things I enjoy!

Photography

I have been doing amature photography on and off since I was around 10 years old. The photo on the right is one I took! I used to go to the Desert Botanical Gardens in Arizona all the time with my grandmother, who is also a photographer, and we would take pictures together. She is who got me into the hobby and taught me a lot of what I know now.

Art

I started drawing seriously at the age of 14, and practiced consistently from then to around 18, after which I took a break from it. I have drawn here and there since then, but not nearly as frequently as when I first started. I still really enjoy art, and have been gathering supplies to begin painting traditionally (I mainly have experience making digital art). The feeling of creating something from nothing is one I really enjoy, and is a bit part of why I enjoy programming so much as well. Here is one of my pieces on the right.

Gaming

I really enjoy video games, and in particular competitive ones. I used to compete (not at the pro level) on teams, and even coached some less experienced players on my game of choice. The reason I initially wanted to learn how to program is because I loved games so much, and wanted to be able to make my own. Now I enjoy programming as a whole, though if I get the opportunity to make games, I would snap it up in a heartbeat. I also enjoy non-video games, like DnD and Magic: the Gathering!
